which summer?

Sometime in 2019 we saw Farmbot online and figured it would be a fun project to design something similiar for our back yard. I saw some opportunity for condensing down some of the cabling with a more interesting main board, but decided to keep the first run simple with plans to make upgrade on future spins. One idea was to remove the RaspPi and coprocessor and replace both with an FPGA with a soft core (perhaps Artix 7 Series w/MicroBlaze) or TI's AM335x Sitara running Linux. Both of these paths would be pretty challenging for me, so the goal was to keep most of the core ICs the same to familiarize with the ecosystem.

Tragedy 1


We both left our jobs and each moved to a different state for new challenges.

Tragedy 2

Covid


Starting new jobs during the pandemic was not on the list of desired challenges. Eventually things became more stable in day-to-day life and work started to pick back up on the schematic.
